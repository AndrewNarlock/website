IAS UW Alumni Newsletter Redesign

This project was finished as an assignment for my first Advanced Web Design course at the University of Washington. The goal of the project was to redesign the UWT Alumni Online Newsletter as shown in the first image. The project was completed by three teamates and myself by implementing UX design and content management strategies discussed during class.

The second image is a screenshot of the final redesign that was built using WordPress. The final design was a dramatic improvement from the original in nearly every aspect. The content and pages on the website were completely restructured in order to improve navigation and user experience across the website. We were able to use official images and UW colors as a sort of brand guide for the design.

One of the main improvements to the site was the layout of the content. The original website included several articles that needed to be organized into appropriate pages and lists. The third image shows a screenshot of a page dedicated to articles written about UW Alumni.

We were also responsible for building contact and about pages which included links to a variety of UW social media accounts. This was an important improvement to the website as it allows users to further connect with the owners of the website.

The last image is a screenshot of the first sitemap that my team constructed. The goal of this design document is to strategize and consider how the pages and navigation will be laid out across the site in the most effective way. It proved to be one of many useful tools for improving the overall user experience of the website.

Thanks for visiting!

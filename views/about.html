About Narlock Web Design:

Narlock Web Design is a project dedicated to improving user experiences across the web through a variety of evolving strategies. NWD aims to provide high quality web design and developement services to help businesses, communities, and people improve communication across the internet.

About Me:

My name is Andrew Narlock and I graduated from the University of Washington in 2021 with a Bachelors in Communications. My studies focused on understanding and practicing modern UX web design strategies and principles. Since graduating, I have continued my studies in web design and hope to deepen my understanding of the internet and computers in general.

I am passionate about studying the world and finding new perspectives to see it through. In my free time I practice math, pilosophy, and computer science. I have been interested in computers ever since I was a kid playing games on my parents laptop. I hope to continue my studies and to aquire a better understanding of the world and the people in it. Computers and the internet have become incredibly important tools for humans everywhere and I hope to be able to contribute to their developement in a meaningful way.

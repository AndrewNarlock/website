New Gen Wrestling Club Website Design

The goal of this project was to design a bold and welcoming website for New Gen Wrestling Club. I started out by meeting with the client to understand what they need from a new website, as this would be their first. They really just needed something to share information about the club, such as scheduling and membership costs. The strategy for the new design was to build something inviting for new students that also represents the winning spirit of the school. The finished website can be found here: New Gen Wrestling Club. (some client information has been changed in this copy of the design)

After meeting with my client I was able to utilize the information and brand guide to start working on the content strategy. A site map was created to help me visualize how I wanted to break the information into different pages. I also was able to get a logo and color palette which made some of the planning simpler as I had an idea for the aesthetics they were looking for.

Some basic wireframes and low fidelity prototypes were constructed to help figure out the spacing and layout of the content.

Three different prototypes were designed so that the client had some choice and could point out specific details that they wanted from each design. While the client ultimately wanted the first prototype, having multiple paths available made it easier to determine where they wanted to go with the design. The first prototype used bolder colors and backgrounds while the other designs got progressively more reserved. Overall, the client was very satisfied with the results and was happy that we were able to meet and iterate over the designs in order to come to a final result.
